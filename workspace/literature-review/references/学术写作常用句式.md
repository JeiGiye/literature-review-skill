## 连接与过渡词

### 递进关系
- 此外、另外、同时、再者
- 更重要的是、值得注意的是
- 进一步来说、在此基础上
- 不仅如此、而且

### 转折关系
- 然而、但是、不过、尽管如此
- 相反地、与之不同的是
- 虽然如此、尽管……仍然
- 与此形成对比的是

### 因果关系
- 因此、因而、所以、据此
- 这就导致、这说明
- 这表明、这意味着
- 由于……因此

### 补充说明
- 具体来说、例如、比如
- 即、也就是说、换言之
- 特别是、尤其是
- 包括但不限于

### 总结归纳
- 总之、综上所述、总的来说
- 简言之、概言之
- 总体来看、从整体上看
- 总的来说、总的来说

## 表达方式规范

### 客观陈述
- ✅ 本研究结果表明……
- ✅ 数据显示……
- ✅ 分析发现……
- ❌ 我认为……
- ❌ 我们觉得……

### 引用证据
- ✅ 正如张三(2020)所指出的，"……"
- ✅ 据李四(2021)的研究，……
- ✅ 实验数据显示，……
- ❌ 大家都知道……

### 学术用语
- ✅ 表明、显示、证实、揭示
- ✅ 提出、探讨、分析、论述
- ✅ 有助于、促进、推动
- ❌ 超级、非常、特别、极其

---

## 避免的常见错误

### 口语化表达
- ❌ 这个方法挺好的
- ✅ 该方法具有良好的性能

### 主观判断
- ❌ 这种研究没有什么意义
- ✅ 该研究的实践价值有限

### 绝对化表述
- ❌ 这项研究是完美的
- ✅ 该研究在……方面表现优异

### 模糊不清
- ❌ 有人说……
- ✅ 张三(2020)等学者认为……

### 逻辑混乱
- ❌ 因为A，而且B，所以C
- ✅ 由于A和B，因此C

## 英文常用句式（针对英文文献综述）

### Introduction
- In recent years, ... has emerged as a research hotspot in the field of ...
- With the development of ..., ... has been widely applied in ...
- ... has important theoretical significance and practical value in ...
- Addressing the problem of ... is crucial for ...

### Literature Review
- Domestic and international scholars have conducted extensive research on ...
- ... is one of the core issues in the field of ... and has received widespread attention.
- Current research on ... mainly focuses on the following aspects.
- Significant progress has been made in ... through existing studies, but ... remains to be explored.

### Analysis and Discussion
- Synthesizing the existing literature reveals that ... has become the mainstream research direction.
- Most studies confirm that ... has good effects in ...
- ... is currently a key technical bottleneck in the field of ...
- Existing studies mainly adopt ... methods, but there are certain limitations.

### Conclusion
- In summary, this paper systematically reviews the research progress of ...
- Existing studies demonstrate that ... has broad application prospects in ...
- The key to ... lies in solving the problem of ...
- Multiple studies have confirmed the effectiveness of ..., but further verification is still needed.
